Overview

This page contains the materials of the workshop entitled "Modular Software Infrastructure for Excited State Dynamics". The workshop was held at the University at Buffalo, June 8-11, 2018 under the umbrella of MolSSI-supported workshops. The workshop discussed the state of the existing methods for dynamics of electronic and electron-nuclear dynamics (ranging from fully quantum approaches to various semi-classical, and quasi-classical metohods). The existing packages and implementations of such methods have been discussed, the range of their potential applicaitons and impact demonstrated, and the future needs identified.
