Welcome the the Quantum Dynamics Hub! This portal hosts a number of software for quantum dynamics simulaitons, including nonadiabatic molecular dynamics, exact wavepacket propagation, semi-classical methods for quantum nuclear dynamics, and so on.

This portal is relatively new, and therefore is under construction. So for, you should be able to navigate to the websites of some specialized programs (see the navigation bar menu).
